IRON MAN GOES INTERNATIONAL

With INVINCIBLE IRON MAN already a wild success, Shellhead will receive a second series next week: INTERNATIONAL IRON MAN. Writer Michael Bendis, who reunites with longtime creative collaborator Alex Maleev on the book, vows the new title will change Tony Stark on a fundamental level and show fans a side of the character that’s never been explored before.

“There’s so much to Tony’s character, just so much always going on with him,” explains Bendis. “He doesn’t live the life of a normal person; he’s actually living a bunch of lives all at the same time, in a way. So our plans for the character are that as we have this international landscape, as it continues, the stories get more personal throughout the book.” Bendis referred back to his previous work with artist Maleev and said that readers can expect more of that deep dive into Tony Stark’s psyche with the new book: “My work with Alex, we tend to really dig into a character and strip down what makes them tick, deconstructing how they are the way they are and why they are the way they are. INTERNATIONAL IRON MAN will be the place where we do that.”
